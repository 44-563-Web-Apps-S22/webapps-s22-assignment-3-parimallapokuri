RRR Restaurant

RRR restaurant is very famous in Vijayawada, where we can find delicious food items like Chicken Biryani, Prawns Biryani, Mutton Pulao. There are lot of argicultural crops around that restaurant, if we go to the restaurant we can experience a good scenery with delicious food

Vijayawada Airport, Gannavaram

  1. From Airport there is busstop
  2. Take a bus at airport and take a bus ticket to Benz circle
  3. While travelling from Airport to Benz circle we need to cross the krishna River
  4. We have to take a boat to cross the River where we can find a place Bhavani Island
  5. From Bhavani Island we need to take a Pulic Bus to reach Benz circle
  6. After reaching Benz circle, There is restaurant called Swagruha Foods
  7. After entering into restaurant, they will give you menu
  8. There we can find different types of delicious foods
